Privacy Policy

Precision Labs: Tuner & Metronome  ·  Alien Guitar Secrets  ·  Last updated: 30 June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Precision Labs: Tuner & Metronome app ("the App"), published by Alien Guitar Secrets (Robert Lobasso), handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The App does not require you to create an account.

2. Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device's microphone solely to perform real-time pitch detection for the chromatic tuner feature. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or sent to any server.

3. Advertising (Free Tier)

The free tier of the App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised ads in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. You can review Google's practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

Upgrading to Precision Labs Pro removes all advertisements.

4. Subscription Purchases

In-app purchases and subscription management are handled by RevenueCat. Payment processing is performed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) directly. We do not receive or store your payment card details. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

5. Analytics

We do not use any analytics or crash-reporting SDKs in the App beyond what is provided by default by the Apple App Store and Google Play Store platforms.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
